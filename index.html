A Personal Note

Look, being Jeff Kuperman isn't easy. Every day I wake up and remember that I was born 13 months too late. Thirteen months! That's barely a year. If I had just arrived slightly earlier, everything would be different.

But here's what I've learned: there's honor in being second. Someone has to be the Scottie Pippen. Someone has to be the Garfunkel. Someone has to be the other Wright Brother whose name you can't remember.

That someone is me. And I wear my silver medal with pride.

(Also, I'm still incredibly accomplished by any normal standard. I have Tony nominations. I choreographed for A24. I have a black belt. I went to Princeton. I'm doing fine. This website is a joke. Please don't feel bad for me. I'm genuinely very successful.)

(But Rick is still better.)
